A little bit about me...

Summing up the little details that make up who you are can be tough, but I’ll try my best..

Born in South Korea, I traveled with my parents to New Jersey with me as a little tyke, who could barely speak two words of English. I remember I had an especially hard time with the word–”diamond”. (Anybody else out there who shared my pain?!)

Selfie at Powell Library

Picked up the language pretty quickly, and before I could walk, I was dancing and singing–jiving to the tunes of Mary Poppins, Michael Jackson, and any funky records I could get my chubby little hands on.

I found a passion for biochemistry, and made it my objective to become a doctor, without realizing that the job title was a life style and not just an avenue for financial comfort. I managed to turn a blind eye to this until recently, I realized that coding was an avenue to something beyond just the monetary. It was a tool to build on top of the cultural phenomenon that was taking the world by storm: the Internet. Not only was this “hip and groovy” (or “bomb” depending on the generation you find comfort in), it was something I genuinely enjoyed and made time melt away. Ruby was my first language, and suddenly time was measured in lines of code; the day began and ended with DEF and END keywords.

Fortunately, I realized that coding my day away was nothing without the people I could share my efforts with. I love communicating with people who share my passions, and am humbled and extended by those who are different and radical. I love mixing with people to create something.

This website is the digital home and mental repository of an explorer of web development and music. Feel free to reach out to me. I enjoy giving autographs and faking them (if you are ever in desperate need of George Clooney’s.. I do a great one).
